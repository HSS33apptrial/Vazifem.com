## Typography

- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)
- **Subset:** latin-ext (for Turkish characters)

## Border Radius

- Cards: `16px` (rounded-2xl)
- Small elements: `12px` (rounded-xl)
- Pills/badges: `9999px` (full round)
- Icons: `12px`

## Instagram Post Size

- Feed post: 1080 x 1080 px
- Story: 1080 x 1920 px
